Favorite Podcast Episodes

Derek Sivers on All the Hacks

Derek Sivers is someone I've admired for a while now, ever since I first heard him on the Tim Ferriss podcast. In this episode he's having a wide-ranging discussion with Chris, and it's given me a lot of ideas for changes I want to go further in making with my own life.

Francis Tapon on Zero to Travel

This episode is not just about where to go and what to see in eastern Europe, but more like a philosophy on travel in general, and changes it can make in your life.

Bill Perkins on All the Hacks

The moment I finished this episode, I immediately got the the Audiobook (of the same name) and finished it within a few days. This has inspired me to rethink my life, and really evaluate where I put my time, what makes me happy, and how money factors into that.
